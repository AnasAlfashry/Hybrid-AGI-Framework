# Hybrid Cognitive Frameworks for Safe and Contextual Artificial General Intelligence

This repository contains the research paper submitted to AI Journey 2025, proposing a novel hybrid architecture for Artificial General Intelligence (AGI) that integrates:

- Neural and symbolic reasoning
- Contextual learning dynamics
- Embedded ethical decision-making protocols

## 📄 Paper Summary

**Title:** Hybrid Cognitive Frameworks for Safe and Contextual AGI  
**Author:** Anas Alfashry  
**Date:** July 2025  
**Status:** Submitted to AI Journey 2025 Conference  

The paper discusses the challenges in AGI development and introduces a three-layered methodology for safer, culturally aware, and ethically grounded AGI systems.
